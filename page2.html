MAIN CHARACTERS
Crisostomo Ibarra
  • He is an educated Filipino.
  • He represents the youth's idealism.
  • He is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra.
  • He is the love interest of Maria Clara.
Maria Clara
  • She is a beautiful and moral Filipina.
  • She represents purity and innocence.
  • She is the daugther of Kapitan Tiago and Pia Alba.
  • She is the love interest of Crisostomo Ibarra.

SIDE CHARACTERS
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • He is a rich and well-respected landowner.
  • He represents the Filipino elite's want for progress.
  • He is the father of Crisostomo Ibarra.
Kapitan Tiago
  • He is a wealthy and well-respected Filipino businessman who hosts extravagant parties.
  • He represents the elite Filipinos who oppress other Filipinos in order to get what they want.
  • He is the father of Maria Clara.
Tiya Isabel
  • She is a very religious Filipina.
  • She represents the traditional customs of the Filipinos during the Spanish colonial Period.
  • She raised Maria Clara.
Sisa
  • She is a poor Filipina.
  • She represents the poor and oppressed Filipinos during the Spanish colonial period.
  • She is the mother of Crispin and Basilio.
Basilio
  • He is a responsible and clever boy.
  • He represents the youth's determination during the Spanish colonial period.
  • He is the son of Sisa and Pedro.
  • He is the older brother of Crispin.
Crispin
  • He is a young and quiet boy.
  • He represents the poor and how oppressed they are by the elite.
  • He is the son of Sisa and Pedro.
  • He is the younger brother of Basilio.
Pilosopo Tasio
  • He is a wise and philosophical man.
  • He represents the Filipinos who were against the rule of the Spanish.
  • He is the mentor of Crisostomo Ibarra.

ANTAGONISTS
Padre Damaso
  • He is an Spanish priest.
  • He is the main antagonist of Noli Me Tangere.
  • He is physically abusive and corrupt.
  • He represents the Spanish's abuse and discrimination against the Filipinos.
